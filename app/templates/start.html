Get started

This program will produce two separate rankings of all your material topics based on the answers that you've provided in the questionnaire.

The first set of rankings is produced by calculating the cost, impact on the organization, and external impacts of all the material topics. You can choose how many material topics you want to see in this ranking.

The second set of results is produced by ranking the results solely based off of the score that they received from the questionnaire.

Just follow the prompts displayed on the screen and the program will take you through a questionnaire to help you determine which topics are material.

To determine material topics, stakeholders must answer the same set of (often very many) questions for many different topics. This program helps you save a lot of time by gradually diminishing the number of material topics that are being asked about to prioritize the most important ones. It also produces two sets of results that can be used to cross-check with manually calculated topics.
